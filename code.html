Functional Automated Data Driven Tests
DreamWorks Animation SKG
I recently completed an internship at DreamWorks, where I had the awesome opportunity to work with their Crowds Department! I worked on designing and writing a set of automated tests for the tools in the Crowds pipeline.

Cognitive Science Experiments
Perception & Action Lab, RPI
I worked as a programmer in RPI's Perception & Action Lab from May 2013 - May 2014. During my time there, I contributed to a variety of VR experiments studying human perception, reaction, and locomotion. These experiments were written in C++, Python, and OpenGL and linked to Vicon Motion Capture and Intersense Motion Tracking systems for data collection.

Pipeline Design
Perception & Action Lab, RPI
I developed a pipeline and tutorials for members of the P&A Lab to create animated versions of their experiments. This involved teaching them basic skills in Autodesk Maya and MotionBuilder, such as how to apply motion capture data to a 3D character. I also wrote a MEL script to transfer the recorded behavior of an obstacle in an experiment to its counterpart in the animated version.
Examples of how this tool was used can be found here (coming soon).
